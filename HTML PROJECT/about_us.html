Magma High School
-About Us-

Every year, we receive young boys and girls who have excelled at the
primary level. We take them through a transformational process to make
them useful young people. Men who are strong in body, mind and
character and will serve their fellows faithfully.
People who benefit the world by making them valuable. This is our
mission. The school has various programmes and activities both
academic and non-academic that run throughout the year all meant to
prepare the young students to the world. With the best teachers around,
knowledge is passed, character is learnt and values are inculcated.
Being a christian based institutions, students are also nourished
spiritually everyday.
